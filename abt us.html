"FOR SHIVA SUITS AND SAREES WAS NEVER SIMPLY A STORE,
BUT A WELL-CONTEMPLATED THOUGHT. "
-Manish Kumar
As pure as the sacred word, Shiva, was the vision with which
Shiva Suits and Sarees was born. Founded by two entrepreneurial brothers,
Manish Kumar and Raj Kumar, its inception was based on a single aim —
bringing in trust into the then highly monetized textile industry.
The two brothers were determined to not just change this tarnished
image but also bring in the best quality ethnic wear for every woman,
from she who is picking out her daily wear clothes to the bride,
creating her wedding trousseau and picking out that perfect attire
for her wedding day. And the rest, as they say, is history.

With a 500 square feet store located in Ambala’s traditional cloth
market, the journey of Shiva Sarees began in the late 80’s.
Upholding the values with which Shiva Sarees was created,
simple yet sturdy managerial as well as operational systems
were put in place, a significant one among them being the
Label price, that is, the One-price mechanism. Entirely
up-to-dated variety was presented and remarkable service was
provided to the customers. In almost no time, the brand Shiva Suits
and Sarees became synonymous with panache and trust. As could be expected,
expansion came in next! The result of all the hard work was now visible.
The 500 square feet store now grew to a sprawling shopping centre,
covering an area of 1 Lac square.
