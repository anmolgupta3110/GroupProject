Bridal Gowns – Talk Of The Town

Wedding is an auspicious moment in every bride’s life and they plan it since their childhood.
Yes, Wedding outfits are the star of it in today’s era youngsters are being experimental and do not hesitate from making
bold statements in their wedding and add-on parties.
In this century everyone wants to stand out whether it’s with help of color, attire, accessorize. Theme,
event entry, and dance performance have become new rituals of Indian weddings.
Reception is a platform where you meet first time with the world as a married couple hence making a
statement is the aim of every new-be-bride.
Among various alternatives from bold lehengas to banarse sarees, an attire that can come out in a
very bold way yet is very intriguing is a trail gown.
Your journey from being your dad’s princes to becoming queen of your soul mate, your choice of attire
can sing it in a mesmerizing way.
It can be a statement piece that will a we your near and dear ones and also help you make a grand entry.
Red is a color that is part of the Indian wedding in one or another outfit it makes its way. A Redtrail
gown with full handwork can be your statement piece and help people see your transaction from Miss to MRS.




A maroon trail gown with complete handwork and graceful detailing can be head turner piece for your reception.
Full sleeves with detailing and a beautiful back to complete the look.




The bride who wants to go for pastel shades or light shades will love this traditional trail style gown.
It also has a dupatta that helps you style the way you want.




For brides who want to stand out and try some other shades this complete hand embroidered piece with a
tail is a good pick.
This trail style gown can be a perfect outfit for your special day and your partner in crime that
can help you steal the heart of your soul mate once again leaving them awestruck by you.
